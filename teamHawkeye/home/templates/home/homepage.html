About

This site provides Chicago residents with accurate data tracking and analysis of all Chicago service requests that were created in the year 2018 and later. Once an account has been created, Chicago residents will have the ability to report new service requests, view all existing requests, check the status of existing requests, and be stay informed about all newly created and completed service requests in their area via an autonomous notification system.
